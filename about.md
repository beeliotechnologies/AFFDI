PREAMBLE
Understanding that working together the only way through which communities have managed to improve on their social economic standards, we the founder members of Alliance For Fundamental Development Initiative decided to pool our resources together so as to achieve a common goal.
The group shall therefore focus on promotion of agriculture, education and improving of health and sanitation services in order to enhance their household incomes for improved livelihood and sustainable development. 
ARTICLE 1: NAME OF THE ORGANISATION
1	.1 The name of the organization shall be called Alliance for Fundamental Developmental Initiative (AFFDI) herein referred to as the group,
1.2	The group shall be non-partisan and shall be recognized as a community based self-help group 
1.3	The organization shall operate on its core values of; respect for one another, transparency, accountability and non-discrimination.
ARTICLE 2: LOCATION OF THE ORGANISATION.
 2.1 The group shall have its offices in Tirinyi Town Council, Kibuku District.

ARTICLE 8: ORGANIZATION MEETINGS. 
8.1 There shall be three types of meetings to be conducted;
	8.1.1 	General Assembly Meetings which shall be sitting under the following procedures, 
(i)	The Organization shall meet weekly to fulfill the savings and credit obligation and after every one year to fulfill the annual general assembly meeting.
(ii)	Notice indicating the time, place and agenda for annual meetings shall be given in writing to ali members at least 7 days in advance.
(iii)	All members shall attend the annual general assembly meeting and the voting shall be one member  one vote provided the members voting rights are adhered to.
(iv)	The quorum for the annual general assembly meeting shall be 2/3 of all the delegates entitled to   attend the meeting.
8.1.2 	Executive Committee Meetings which shall be managed under the following procedures: -	 
(i) The executive committee shall meet every month.
(i) A meeting of the Executive Committee may also be called at any time if there is a request by 3   members of the Executive Committee or by the chairperson.
(iii)	The chairperson shall preside over all meetings of both the General Assembly and the Executive Committee and in his or her absence the vice chairperson.
(iv)	In case of the chairperson and the vice chairperson, the members present shall elect one person amongst themselves to preside over such a meeting.
(v)	3/4 members on the Executive Committee shall constitute the quorum for the executive committee   meetings.
(vi)	Any resolution of the Executive Committee shall be passed by consensus.
	8.1.3 	Extra Ordinary Meetings which shall be conducted under the following procedures; 
	 	(i) The chairperson through the general secretary shall call for extra ordinary organization meetings.
(ii)These meetings shall be called at a request of 1/3 of the organization members or majority of the members on the Executive Committee,
(iii) A 3 days' notice for such meetings shall be given to the members of the group.

ARTICLE 11: RELATIONSHIPS WITH OTHER LEGAL BODIES AND GOVERNMENT.
ALLIANCE FOR FUNDAMENTAL DEVELOPMENT INITIATIVE (AFFDI) shall cooperate and maintain its relationship through its administrative arms by a way of establishing mutual understanding and relationship and participate in all development undertaking, Local, National and International in nature provided they are in line with the organization's vision, mission and objectives.
ARTICLE 12: SAFE CUSTODY OF PROPERTY
The books, plans, official papers, documents and correspondences of the group shall be in the direct control of the executive members.
ARTICLE 13: REPEAL AND AMENPMENT OF THE ORGANIZATION CONSTITUTION 
The group's constitution may be modified, enlarged, abridged or added from time to time by special resolutions passed by two thirds majority vote of the members present in person at the General Assembly meeting provided notice for such a meeting was communicated to the members in accordance with the provisions of the organization's  constitution.
ARTICLE 14: DISSOLUTION. 
The group shall not be dissolved except by a resolution passed at the Annual General Meeting or Special General Meeting attended by two third of the total registered members. 
On the dissolution of the group, the Annual General Meeting or Special Meeting shall at the same sitting, instruct the Executive Committee to take such action as will be necessary for the liquidation of the organization's assets and sharing of liabilities.
ARTICLE 15: AFFILIATION.
The group may by resolution affiliate with anybody concerned with its interests or in line with its objectives within and  outside Uganda.
ARTICLE 16: ARBITRATION. 
In case of any differences in interpretation of this constitution, anything contained herein or members dispute and or conflicts, the rule or regulation their under shall be decided by an independent, eminent person whose ruling on any matters affecting the concerned parties is final and binding. His ruling shall be in respect to the point of contention  therein referred to the person or body in trust.
ARTICLE 17: INDE MNITY.
Any person, party, manager or officer of ALLIANCE FOR FUNDAMENTAL DEVELOPMENT INITIATIVE shall be indemnified of the funds and liabilities of the group whether the charges accrue from defending the interests of the group or personal, unless such a person is in fault in connection therewith. 
ARTICLE 18: INTERPRETATIONS OF ARTICLES OF THE ORGANIZATION.
The Executive Committee and Sub Committees in consultation with the legal adviser of the group shall give the final interpretation on articles of the Organization.
ARTICLE 20: PENALTIES FOR BREACH OF THE CONSTITUTION.
Any member or committee violating duty of the provision of this constitution shall be penalized for such a breach, provided the penalty does not go against the laws of the land. The penalties may include among others the following: -
(i)	Cautioning in writing
(ii)	Fining	 
(iii)	Exposure
(iv)	Paying back [Debt/property recovery]
(v)	Suspension
(vi)	Termination
(vii)	Expulsion
